# Hyundai Solar (HD Hyundai Energy Solutions) - Module Reference

## HiN-T440NF(BK)

440W N-Type TOPCon Bifacial Black Module | 108 Half-Cut Cells

### Product Overview

- **Series:** NF(BK)
- **Model:** HiN-T440NF(BK)
- **Wattage:** 440W
- **Cell Technology:** N-Type TOPCon Monocrystalline, Bifacial, 16BB
- **Cell Count:** 108 (6 x 18 half-cut)
- **Glass:** Dual glass -- 2.0mm AR-coated semi-tempered front and rear
- **Frame:** Black anodized aluminum alloy
- **Connector:** Staubli MC4 genuine / compatible, IP68
- **Cable Length:** 1200mm (47.2 in) positive and negative
- **Max System Voltage:** 1500 VDC (IEC)
- **Series Fuse Rating:** 30A

### Notes for BOM / Installation

- Bifacial gain potential: 5-30% depending on ground reflectivity (albedo)
- Same dimensions as SEG Solar SEG-440-BTD-BG -- 1722 x 1134 x 30mm
- 30mm frame compatible with standard IronRidge UFO mid/end clamps
- Slightly lighter than SEG (22.68 kg vs 24.0 kg) due to glass-glass construction differences
- TOPCon cell technology -- higher efficiency potential vs standard PERC
- Fire classification: Type 29
