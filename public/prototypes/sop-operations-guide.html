Automation Legend & System Map

Every action in this SOP is tagged to show what's automated, what's manual, and what triggers downstream automations.

Tag Reference

AUTO

Happens automatically via HubSpot workflow. No human action needed.

MANUAL

Requires a team member to take action — update a field, make a call, upload a doc.

TRIGGER

Your manual action kicks off downstream automations. You act, then the system reacts.

ZUPER SYNC

Data synced from Zuper Pro field software. Update in Zuper → auto-syncs to HubSpot.

CHECKLIST

Completed by submitting a Zuper checklist (survey complete, construction complete, inspection failure). Submission syncs to HubSpot and triggers automations.

CALCULATED

Auto-calculated timing metric. Computes when date fields are populated.

NOTIFICATION

Auto-sent customer SMS + Email notification triggered by a status change.

Site Survey Complete Checklist

After the field survey, the surveyor or Site Survey Lead fills out the Site Survey Complete Checklist in Zuper (synced to HubSpot's Center Panel → Zuper & Operations tab). This checklist captures:

Checklist ItemWhy It Matters
Sales Change Order Needed?If the survey reveals conditions that require a contract change (system size, equipment, price), flag here. Stops the design process until resolved.
Specifics Needed for Design?Capture anything the design team needs: roof pitch measurements, shading notes, attic access, conduit run requirements, etc.
Notes for Design TeamFree-text field for surveyor observations. This is the primary handoff mechanism from field to design.
MPU Required?If the meter panel / main panel upgrade is needed, note here. MPU work must be scheduled separately and may delay interconnection.

Activity Types You'll See

1. Pinned Notes

Automated notes pinned to the top of the feed. The most common is the Permitting & Interconnection Thread — a running thread with AHJ/Utility info and team comments. Created by the "Automated Project Thread" workflow.

Use the pinned thread for ongoing permitting discussion. Add comments to the pinned note rather than creating separate notes — this keeps everything in one thread.

2. Automated Tasks

Tasks created by numbered workflow sequences and assigned to specific team members. These follow the project lifecycle:

6. Deal Activity (Stage Changes)

Logged automatically when the deal moves between pipeline stages. Shows the exact transition: "moved from Ready To Build to Construction". Click "View details" for the full property change log.

8. Manual Notes

Notes entered by team members. Common examples: Install Reports from crew leads, project update notes from PMs, customer interaction summaries.
